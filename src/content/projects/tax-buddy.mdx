Tax tooling for individuals in India tends to be either bloated enterprise software or confusing government portals. Tax Buddy is a more focused attempt — a containerised platform where the frontend and backend are clearly separated and the local development experience is reproducible from a single `docker compose up`.

The engineering effort here was mostly about getting the deployment and architecture right: structured issue tracking, contribution guidelines, deployment documentation, and a workflow that supports iterative development without accumulating technical debt.

**What I'd improve next:** real tax calculation logic beyond the current scaffolding, and a proper test suite before adding more features.
